About


Welcome to TIAMAT

The Revolutionary Token designed to transform the lottery industry through blockchain technology.
Tiamat (TMT) is the governance and utility token of our innovative lottery platform,providing unparalleled transparency, fairness, and winning opportunities.

Key Features of Tiamat:


Transparency: All jackpot and winning transactions are publicly verifiable on the blockchain,ensuring complete transparency.


Fairness: With a guaranteed high number of weekly winners,90% of the jackpot amounts are returned as winnings, making the lottery system more rewarding and equitable.


Utility: TMT is used for participating in weekly lotteries, filling jackpots, and retrieving winnings, driving significant weekly trading volume and balanced market demand.


Innovation: Our future plans include developing our own blockchain, creating a proprietary wallet, and launching an exchange platform to enhance TMT's utility and value.


Security: Robust security measures, including easy-to-complete Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) protocols, ensure a safe and compliant environment for all users.


Sustainability: Operating on the Solana blockchain, Tiamat processes a large number of transactions efficiently while minimizing its carbon footprint.


Join us in revolutionizing the lottery experience with Tiamat, where transparency, fairness, and innovation come together to create a truly groundbreaking platform.
